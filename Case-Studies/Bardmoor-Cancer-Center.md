# Bardmoor Cancer Center  
Oncology Treatment Platform  
Live Site: https://bardmoorcc.com/



## Overview

Bardmoor Cancer Center is a Florida-based oncology treatment center focused on personalized cancer care, patient education, and structured treatment presentation.

The objective was to build a content-driven oncology platform that clearly communicates treatment pathways while supporting patient intake and long-term SEO growth.

Unlike multi-network systems, this platform was engineered as a highly structured single-location treatment hub.
