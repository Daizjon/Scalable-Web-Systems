# cCARE Network  
**California Cancer Associates for Research & Excellence**  
Live Site: https://ccare.com/



## Overview

cCARE is one of the largest private oncology networks in California, serving multiple regional practices under a unified brand architecture.

The platform functions as a multi-location healthcare system, supporting:

- Multiple practice entities
- Structured service-line content
- Patient and referring physician workflows
- Network-wide governance and compliance

This was engineered as a scalable digital platform rather than a standalone website.
