# Urology Partners  
**URL:** https://urologypartners.net/  
**Type:** Regional Urology Practice Platform  
**CMS:** WordPress + Elementor  
**Industry:** Healthcare / Urology  

---

## Overview

Urology Partners is a regional urology practice providing comprehensive care for both men and women, including men’s health, women’s health, general urologic services, and patient education.

The platform supports multi-location visibility, provider-focused content, and structured service segmentation while maintaining a clean, accessible user experience for patients seeking specialty care close to home.

---

## Scale

- ~25 Pages  
- ~41 Blog Posts  
- Multi-location structure  
- Multi-specialty segmentation (Men’s Health / Women’s Health)

## Objectives
